What is Sleep Debt and Why Should I Calculate It?

Sleep debt (or sleep deficit) is the accumulated difference between the amount of sleep you need and the amount you actually get. According to sleep scientists, having chronic sleep debt can severely impact your health and focus.

FAQ: Recovering From Sleep Debt

Q: How much sleep debt can I safely have? A: Experts recommend keeping your accumulated sleep debt below 10 hours, but zero debt is ideal. Use this tool daily!

Q: Can I catch up on sleep debt? A: Yes, you can recover gradually, often by adding an extra hour of sleep on non-work days. Use our calculator to track your progress.
